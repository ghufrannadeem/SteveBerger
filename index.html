Georgia Psychological Associates!

Expert Clinical Psychologist in Manhattan

Georgia Psychological Associates is a specialty clinic with an exclusive focus on assessment and evaluation. Established in 1997 by Steven Berger, Ph.D, the clinic has grown to include over 15 clinicians and support staff.

We conduct over 2000 evaluations yearly for Social Security Disability, Division of Family and Childrenâ€™s Services, Division of Juvenile Justice, Georgia Advocacy Office, and Vocational Rehabilitation. We also work extensively in the private sector conducting neuropsychological, Psycho Educational, forensic, ADHD, Autism and many other types of evaluations.

Our referral sources include attorneys, neurologists, psychiatrists, pediatricians, school districts, academic consultants, psychical therapists, occupational therapists, speech and language pathologists, and other professionals from more than 25 different counties throughout the state of Georgia.

We strive to work collaboratively and above all are committed to quality and timeliness in order to provide the highest value to all of those with whom we work and serve.
